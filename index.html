We are Lexington Painting

The most versatile, knowledgable, skilled painters on Earth

Lexington Paint is a business built with a family history that dates back to Greece circa 1920s. My grandfather started out making brushes but soon found that painting was a much more lucrative side to the business. He started teaching my father, Spiro Sr., the ins and outs of the painting industry well over 55 years ago.

That tradition found its way to New York City where Spiro Sr. started out only painting residential homes across all five boroughs.

39 years ago, Spiro Jr., with the family traditions passed down, took over the family business.
